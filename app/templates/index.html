GibbsCluster 2.0

Simultaneous alignment and clustering of peptide data

GibbsCluster is a tool for unsupervised alignment and clustering of peptide sequences. The program takes as input a list of peptide sequences and attempts to cluster them into meaningful groups.
Update (Nov 2016): Implements deletions and insertions in the sequence alignment.
